Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Overview

SPRAXXX.ai values privacy as dignity in digital form. This Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how you control your data across the SPRAXXX Nation services (.ai • .com • .tv • .net • .xxx).

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity Data — limited details submitted during Stripe Identity verification (name, date of birth, photo ID). Raw documents are processed by Stripe, not stored by us.
  • Contact Data — your verified email, messages sent via contact forms, and support correspondence.
  • Technical Data — minimal analytics such as browser type and approximate region, collected without third-party trackers.
  • Transaction Data — receipts, payment confirmation tokens, and verification status returned from Stripe.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To issue and validate SPRAXXX Verified Credentials.
  • To maintain secure access to your SPRAXXX-linked services (email, VPN, companion tools).
  • To communicate essential updates or respond to your inquiries.
  • To comply with legal and financial record-keeping requirements.

4. What We Don’t Do

  • No sale or rental of personal information.
  • No advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.
  • No automated profiling that affects rights or access without human review.

5. Data Storage & Security

We store only hashed verification tokens and necessary account metadata on encrypted servers managed by SPRAXXX Nation. Data in transit is protected by TLS 1.3. Access is restricted to authorized maintainers through multi-factor authentication.

6. Data Retention

Verification records are retained only as long as required to maintain an active credential or comply with legal obligations. When no longer needed, they are deleted or anonymized using cryptographic erasure methods.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of your stored data.
  • Correction — update inaccurate or outdated details.
  • Deletion — request permanent removal once obligations end.
  • Portability — export your credential proof to another verified service.

Requests can be made via privacy@spraxxx.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

SPRAXXX.ai uses session storage for essential functions such as remembering signed-in state. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies. You may clear your browser data at any time without losing account integrity.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — identity and payment verification. See Stripe’s Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — handles contact form submissions securely with no resale or advertisement tracking.
  • We may link to other SPRAXXX domains (.tv, .com, .net) that each maintain identical privacy standards.

10. Children’s Privacy

SPRAXXX services are intended for individuals 16 years and older. Verification requires legal consent age in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. International Transfers

All core data is processed and stored in Canada with encrypted backups in jurisdictions providing comparable privacy protections (EU/EEA-aligned). We do not store or process data in countries lacking adequate safeguards.

12. Changes to This Policy
